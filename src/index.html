About

At HappyHatch, we've always believed that remembering the special moments in life should be simple, intuitive, and private. In a world brimming with digital notifications that are too easily dismissed or overlooked, we sought to create a solution that ensures the birthdays of your friends and loved ones are never missed. That's how HappyHatch was born — out of the need for a more direct, engaging, and unobtrusive way to stay on top of these important dates.

Our journey led us to the realization that widgets could serve as an exceptional tool for this purpose. By integrating birthday tracking into widgets, we found a delightful balance between visibility and discretion. HappyHatch's widgets place these events front and center on your device's screen — not lost in a sea of ephemeral notifications, but presented as a constant, gentle reminder each time you glance at your phone.

We designed HappyHatch with privacy at its core. You can track and share birthdays without the need to hand over sensitive data. Your loved ones' special days are stored locally on your device, ensuring that you and only you have control over this personal information. Our commitment to privacy means you enjoy a seamless experience without compromising on security.

HappyHatch is more than just an app — it's a commitment to celebrate life's joys with the people who matter most. It's about making every birthday a little brighter and ensuring that no matter how busy life gets, you're always there for the moments that count.

Join us on this journey and make every day a celebration with HappyHatch.
